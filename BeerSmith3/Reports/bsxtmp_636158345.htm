Mash Notes: Profile created for use with Brewster Beacon. Temperature is kept in the sweet spot (65-67 deg) to obtain conversion of both alpha and beta amylase, resulting in a medium body.
Lower step temperature (63-66 deg) extracts beta amylase, resulting in high attenuation (less sugar extraction) and less body.
Higher step temperature (67-69) extracts alpha amylase, resulting in lower attenuation and more richer body.
Choosing a temperature in between these to extraction points results in a medium body.
